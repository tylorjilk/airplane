MOTOR
	brushless -> need ESC for brushless then
	outrunner -> won't need gearbox and is quiet
	
	Kv = voltage constant
		It is how many rpms the motor turns for each volt applied
	Kt = torque constant = 1355/Kv
		outrunners normally have lower Kt
	Probs want 150-200 Watts/lb
